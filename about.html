Below Decks: A Shield of Tomorrow Podcast

Below Decks is a fan podcast about the Geek & Sundry show Shield of Tomorrow broadcasting every Wednesday at 9:30PM Pacific on Geek & Sundry Twitch and Alpha.

Join the Aux Crew as each week we recap the show, gush over the cast, and examine more in depth the messages and morals tackled on each session. Grab you cream soda and look out for that Badmiral Nash as we go in depth into Shield of Tomorrow and Star Trek!

Follow us on Twitter,  join us in the Discord,  and leave a review on Apple Podcasts or Google Play!
